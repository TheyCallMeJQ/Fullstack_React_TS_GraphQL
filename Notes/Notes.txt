A note on extensions:

Backend init steps:
1. New package.json to backend root
2. devDependencies:
    - typescript
    - @types/node
    - ts-node
3. tsconfig.json to backend root
    $ npx tsconfig.json
4. New scripts to package.json
"scripts":[
    "start": "ts-node src/index.ts"
]
